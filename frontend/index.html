Who We Are

Norshel Day Program supports adults with physical and developmental disabilities. We work with people with:

Primary Support Areas

  • Down Syndrome
  • PDD
  • Autism
  • Prader Willi
  • Cerebral Palsy
  • Bi-polar
  • OCD

Additional Support Areas

  • Riley Day Syndrome
  • Epilepsy
  • FAS
  • Legal blindness
  • Hearing impairments
  • Diabetes

We also support individuals with aggressive and challenging behaviours and those from diverse religions and ethnic backgrounds.

Our History

Norshel has been around since 1983. We first opened our doors when a group of parents and community leaders were concerned about the future of their children upon graduation from the education system. We started out at Norwood school with 4 employees and 1 staff. Our name came from this location (Nor) and sheltered workshop (shel).

Norshel relocated to 1232 Chevrier in 1985. In 1996 the rec program was introduced followed by the summer program in 1998.

In August 2002, Norshel moved to our current location at 20-5 Scurfield Blvd. There are 3 management positions.

We expanded to the Kernaghan location.
